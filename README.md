# FloralHaven

Welcome to the Plant and Flower Shop project! This project aims to create an online platform for selling a variety of plants and flowers, providing users with a delightful shopping experience.

## Table of Contents
- [Introduction](#introduction)
- [Features](#features)

## Introduction

The Plant and Flower Shop is an e-commerce website designed to offer a wide range of plants and flowers for customers who appreciate the beauty of nature. From indoor plants to vibrant flowers, our platform provides a convenient way to explore, choose, and purchase botanical delights.

## Features

- **Responsive Design:** The website is designed to provide a seamless experience across various devices, ensuring accessibility for users on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

- **Product Showcase:** Browse through a diverse collection of plants and flowers with detailed information, including images, prices, and descriptions.

- **User Authentication:** Users can create accounts, log in, and enjoy personalized features such as order history and saved favorites.

- **Easy Navigation:** The navigation bar facilitates easy access to different sections of the website, including the main shop, blog, contact information, and more.

- **Shopping Cart:** Add products to your cart, review your selections, and proceed to a hassle-free checkout process.

- **Blog Section:** Stay updated with gardening tips, plant care advice, and inspiring stories related to the world of plants and flowers.
